## Exceptions — external and third-party material

- **External / legacy talks** (those with `status: external` in their
  `talk.yml`) are hosted elsewhere and keep **their own** license. When it is
  not known, the talk records `license.content: "unknown"`; do not assume
  CC BY-SA for them.
- **Third-party assets** (logos, fonts, images you did not create) remain under
  the license of their respective owners.

When in doubt about a specific talk, check its `talk.yml` and `external.md`.
